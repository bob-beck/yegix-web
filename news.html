    2021

    2020

  • On October 18, the graph server was upgraded, which resulted in a small gap in our graphs.

  • On July 2, Edmonton Transit Service joined YEGIX as a peer.

  • On May 21, The route-servers started applying fairly strict filters based upon various resources including IRRdb and PeeringDB. The route-servers also started filtering prefixes which are RPKI ROA invalid. Thank you YYCIX for the help.

  • On May 15, ARIN fees ($450 USD) donated by Dale Corse / Wolfpaw.

  • On May 14, domain name registration fees ($85 USD) donated by Bob Beck.

  • On May 4, we received 3 new Dell R210 servers. Two to replace our existing route servers, and one for our arouteserver. Thank you Theo de Raadt for the donation.

  • On May 4, we started publishing aggregate traffic graphs. The weekly graph is found on the main page, as well as a larger collection elsewhere. They are updated every 2 minutes.
